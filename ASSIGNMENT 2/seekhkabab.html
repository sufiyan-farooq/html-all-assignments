SEEKH KABAB

Seekh kebabs are soft and succulent, seasoned with various spices such as ginger, garlic, green chilli pepper, powdered chilli and garam masala.
as well as lemon juice, cilantro and mint leaves Sometimes extra fats are added to further enhance the flavor.
Seekh kebabs are typically served with raita, salad, onion slices, lemon wedges or green chutney and eaten with naan or paratha.
